Yatima School Support

Providing quality education and holistic care for orphaned and vulnerable children

Education That Transforms Lives

Our Yatima School program ensures that orphaned and vulnerable children receive quality education in a nurturing environment. We believe education is the most powerful tool to break the cycle of poverty and create sustainable change.

Comprehensive Educational Support

  • Fully-funded primary and secondary education
  • After-school tutoring and homework assistance
  • Computer literacy and digital skills training
  • School supplies, uniforms, and transportation
  • Nutrition program with daily healthy meals
  • Counseling and psychosocial support

Our curriculum goes beyond academics to include life skills, financial literacy, and leadership development. We maintain small class sizes (maximum 25 students) to ensure personalized attention.

Sponsor a Child's Education

Academic Excellence

Our students consistently outperform national averages, with 85% transitioning to secondary education.

Residential Care

Safe dormitories with house parents provide stability for children without family support.

Health & Wellness

Regular medical checkups, dental care, and mental health services ensure children thrive.

Alumni Support

We provide university scholarships and career guidance for our graduates.

Walemavu Support

Specialized care and inclusion programs for children with disabilities

Empowering Children of All Abilities

Our Walemavu (Disability) program provides comprehensive support for children with physical, intellectual, and developmental disabilities. We focus on ability rather than limitation, helping each child reach their full potential.

Specialized Services

  • Individualized education plans (IEPs) tailored to each child's needs
  • Physical, occupational, and speech therapy
  • Assistive devices and mobility aids
  • Social skills and independent living training
  • Parent/caregiver support groups and training
  • Community awareness and anti-stigma campaigns

Our inclusive approach ensures children with disabilities participate fully in all foundation activities alongside their peers. We've helped over 120 children with disabilities gain greater independence since 2015.

Talent Development

Nurturing artistic, athletic, and vocational gifts in vulnerable youth

Discovering and Developing Potential

Our Talent Support program identifies and cultivates the unique abilities of children in our care. Through arts, sports, and vocational training, we help youth build confidence, discipline, and marketable skills.

Talent Pathways

  • Visual arts, music, dance, and drama programs
  • Football (soccer), basketball, athletics, and swimming
  • Chess club and academic competitions
  • Vocational training in carpentry, tailoring, and IT
  • Annual talent showcase and competitions
  • Scholarships for exceptional talent

Several of our alumni have received sports scholarships to universities, exhibited artwork internationally, and launched successful careers in creative fields. Talent development builds self-esteem and opens doors to future opportunities.

Global Outreach

Building international partnerships to expand our impact

Cross-Cultural Collaboration

Our International Outreach program connects NextStep with global partners who share our vision for vulnerable children. Through these collaborations, we exchange knowledge, resources, and support to enhance our programs.

Global Initiatives

  • International volunteer and internship programs
  • Professional exchanges for our staff with partner organizations
  • Global fundraising and child sponsorship network
  • International pen pal and cultural exchange program
  • Participation in global conferences on child welfare
  • Cross-border medical missions and specialist referrals

We currently partner with organizations in 6 countries, benefiting from shared expertise while maintaining our locally-led approach. Our international network helps amplify our impact and advocate for vulnerable children worldwide.
