Our Story

Founded in 2010, NextStep Foundation began with a simple mission: to provide a loving home and quality education to orphaned and vulnerable children in our community. What started as a small initiative has grown into a comprehensive support system that now serves over 50 children annually.

We believe every child deserves safety, education, and the opportunity to discover their unique talents. Our holistic approach addresses physical, emotional, and educational needs while fostering independence and self-worth.

At NextStep, we don't just provide care - we create family. Our children grow up knowing they are valued, capable, and loved.

Our Programs

We offer comprehensive programs designed to meet the diverse needs of the children and individuals we serve.

Orphan Support

We provide a loving home, nutritious meals, quality education, and healthcare to orphaned and vulnerable children. Our residential program ensures stability while we work to reunite children with extended family when possible.

Learn More

Walemavu Support

Our disability inclusion program provides specialized care, therapy, education, and skills training for children and young adults with disabilities. We focus on ability rather than limitation, helping each individual reach their full potential.

Learn More

Talent Development

We identify and nurture the unique talents of our children through arts, sports, music, and vocational training programs. Many of our alumni have gone on to successful careers in their chosen fields.
