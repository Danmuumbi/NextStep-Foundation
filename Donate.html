Sponsor a Child

Our child sponsorship program provides consistent support for a child's education, healthcare, nutrition, and overall wellbeing.

Sponsorship Levels

  • 💰 Basic Support: $30/month - Covers school supplies
  • 🍎 Nutrition Support: $50/month - Provides meals
  • 🏥 Full Support: $100/month - Education, healthcare, and nutrition
  • 🎓 Education Champion: $150/month - Full support plus extracurricular activities

Sponsors receive quarterly updates about their sponsored child and can exchange letters.
