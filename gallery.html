Daily Life at Our Home

June 15, 2024

This photo captures the joyful moments of children during their free time at the foundation. Our home provides a safe and nurturing environment where children can play, learn, and grow together as a family. The bonds formed here often last a lifetime, creating a support system that many of our children lacked before coming to NextStep.
